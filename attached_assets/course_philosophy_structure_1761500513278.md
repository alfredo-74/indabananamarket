## The 90/10 Paradigm Shift

### What Retail Traders See (10%)
- **Charts**: Price movement visualization
- **Indicators**: Derived calculations
- **Patterns**: Historical formations
- **Result**: Trading the effect, not the cause

### What Professionals See (90%)
- **Order Flow**: Who's buying/selling and how much
- **Market Depth**: Supply and demand at each level
- **Absorption**: Large orders being absorbed
- **Volume Profile**: Where transactions actually occur
- **Result**: Trading the cause, not the effect

### The Iceberg Analogy
```
Above Water (10% - Charts):
What retail traders see
│
├─ Price bars/candles
├─ Technical indicators
└─ Chart patterns

Below Water (90% - Order Flow):
What drives price movement
│
├─ Aggressive buy/sell orders
├─ Passive bid/ask depth
├─ Institutional flow
├─ Absorption patterns
└─ Real supply/demand dynamics
```
